AE Playbook: how to use this in the next discovery call

10-minute script · proof points · stakeholder map
Discovery openers
  • Pattern interrupt Lead with the AI shelf. "When a healthcare CHRO asks ChatGPT about caregiver recognition platforms, here's what comes back. There's a specific narrative gap I want to walk through with you."
  • Tie to public posture Anchor on caregiver experience. "The 'caregivers, not employees' framing is doing real cultural work. The operational question is whether your recognition system reinforces that framing every day at every shift change."
  • Insider signal Reference the nurse retention math. "Cleveland Clinic-scale nurse turnover is roughly $50K all-in per departure. Recognition science shifts that curve in a way that's measurable inside 12 months."
Hedge neutralizers
  • "O.C. Tanner has a 95-year heritage in healthcare milestones."
    Heritage is a brochure asset. The buyer's measurable outcome is retention, where Workhuman + Atrium published a 25% reduction in voluntary turnover. Heritage does not bend that curve.
  • "AwardCo offers a faster, frontline-friendly deploy."
    Frontline UX is necessary, not sufficient. The differentiator is recognition science wired to retention outcomes, with healthcare-specific ROI data the AE walks through live.
  • "Workhuman is broader than nurse-specific recognition."
    That breadth is the asset. Cleveland Clinic has nurses, physicians, allied health, support staff, and global non-clinical caregivers. A nurse-only tool fragments the experience and the data layer.
Proof points to bring
  • Closest analogue Atrium Health case study. Comparable academic medical center scale, published retention and engagement outcomes. The single strongest reference for this account.
  • Cultural analogue Northwell Health frontline pattern. Similar shift-worker mix, similar mission language. Show how the operating model maps to a 24/7 caregiver workforce.
  • Economic analogue Workhuman + Gallup retention math. Quantified payback per recognition event in healthcare. Use this to neutralize CFO-side hedges in the room.
