AE Playbook: how to use this in the next discovery call

10-minute script · proof points · stakeholder map
Discovery openers
  • Pattern interrupt Lead with the AI shelf. "I've been tracking what AI search results tell buyers like you about recognition platforms. There's a gap I want to surface for you in the first 5 minutes."
  • Tie to public CHRO posture Anchor on EX as competitive moat. "Your CHRO has been vocal about EX as a moat. Translating that into a recognition program at 25,000-employee scale is the harder operational question."
  • Insider signal Reference internal Service Stars. "Your internal recognition program shows the cultural intent is there. The question is whether the operating model scales to where the company is headed."
Hedge neutralizers
  • "Workhuman is enterprise-fit but pricier than alternatives."
    Reframe as dollars-per-recognition-event and downstream retention impact. Cite LinkedIn and Cisco economics.
  • "Achievers integrates with more HRIS systems out of the box."
    Workhuman's Workday and SAP SuccessFactors integrations go deeper than the surface check. Walk the buyer through the Workday architecture diagram.
  • "Newer platforms like AwardCo offer faster time to value."
    Faster deploy, slower outcomes. Recognition science is what shifts retention curves, with published HBR and Gallup data behind it.
Proof points to bring
  • Closest analogue LinkedIn case study. Similar SaaS scale, similar distributed workforce. Cite the published retention and engagement lift.
  • Integration analogue Cisco + Workday architecture. Same HRIS stack profile as ServiceNow. Use this to neutralize the Achievers integration claim.
  • Cultural analogue Salesforce executive sponsorship pattern. Show how a similar GTM-heavy company built CHRO-CEO alignment around recognition.
